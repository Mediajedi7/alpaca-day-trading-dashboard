The Overall Plan

Goal: prove out four independent automated strategies with fake money against live market data, building a track record before any real capital is considered.

Diversification of edge: each strategy exploits a different inefficiency — political insider signal (Congressional), volatility premium selling (Wheel), liquidity-sweep reversals (Sneaky Pivot), and opening momentum (Gap & Go). They rarely correlate: a bad day for momentum can still be a good day for premium collection.

Risk framework: every strategy passes through risk_guard — $300 intraday loss limit, $5,000 max invested, plus a $500 daily loss cap on the congressional runner. Any halt requires a manual review and an explicit resume_trading.py — nothing auto-resumes after losses.

Operations: everything runs in Docker on the NAS via supercronic, scripts are wrapped by a watchdog that emails on any crash, and code deploys automatically via git pull every 5 minutes. Morning plan, end-of-day results, and a Friday scorecard arrive by email.

What success looks like: consistent positive expectancy per strategy measured in R-multiples, drawdowns contained by the risk caps, and zero unreviewed losing streaks.
